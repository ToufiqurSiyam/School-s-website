About Us

Madhabpur Pilot High School is a secondary-level non-governmental institution located in Madhabpur, Habiganj, within the Sylhet division. The school was established in 1968 and has become well-known for its strong discipline and high-quality teaching. The school is recognized for its affiliation and has an MPO (Monthly Pay Order) status, ensuring that its staff members are paid by the government.

Quick Facts

  • Established: 1968
  • Number of Students: 599
  • Number of Teachers: 19

Facilities

Our school offers a wide range of facilities aimed at enhancing the educational experience for our students. These include:

  • A well-stocked library
  • Modern laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • An ICT lab for computer education
  • A large playground for sports and other physical activities
  • Active involvement in co-curricular activities, including debate competitions and cultural events
